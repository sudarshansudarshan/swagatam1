| 5       | Markov Chains                                                |
| 6       | Introduction to Matrices                                     |

| 8       | Linear Transformations                                       |
| 9       | Matrix Factorization                                         |
| 10      | Applications: Dimensionality Reduction, Recommender Systems, Pagerank, Support Vector Machines |
